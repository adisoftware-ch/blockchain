Blockchain Demo The Demo Simple Blockchain

Blockchain is a database without any central instance. All data is held by the users (Nodes).
The Blockchain technology ensures Availability and Integrity of the data.

  1. Start the App at least twice
  2. Have a look at the 'Node's
  3. Mine the first block
  4. Go to 'Client'; do some transactions
  5. Mine the next blocks
  6. Start a third App
  7. Look at it's Node - it's empty!
  8. Run 'Consensus' - yeahh data is here!
  9. Go on playing around ...

Note: The chain lives only in memory. It lives exactly as long as - worldwide - at least one Node (App) is alive!
